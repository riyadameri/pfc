About The Event

INNOVEX is the flagship event of the Faculty of New Technologies of Information and Communication (FNTIC) at Kasdi Merbah University, Ouargla. Now in its 8th edition, this annual exhibition and conference has become the premier platform for showcasing the innovative final cycle projects of our talented students.

The event brings together students, professors, researchers, and industry professionals to celebrate technological innovation and excellence. With participation from Elitech Club, Electronic Club, and other student organizations, INNOVEX 2025 promises to be the most exciting edition yet, featuring:

  • Exhibition of cutting-edge student projects across various ICT disciplines
  • Keynote speeches from renowned industry leaders and academic experts
  • Interactive technical workshops and hands-on training sessions
  • Panel discussions on emerging technologies and industry trends
  • Networking opportunities with professionals and potential employers
  • Awards ceremony recognizing outstanding projects and innovations
  • Career development sessions and recruitment opportunities

This year's theme: "INNOVEX and the Student of Kasdi Merbah Who Founded It" honors the legacy of student-driven innovation at our university while inspiring the next generation of tech leaders.

Whether you're a student looking to showcase your work, an industry professional seeking fresh talent, or simply passionate about technology, INNOVEX 2025 offers something for everyone.

About FNTIC

The Faculty of New Technologies of Information and Communication (FNTIC) at Kasdi Merbah University, Ouargla is a leading academic institution dedicated to advancing knowledge and innovation in ICT fields. Established in response to the growing demand for expertise in digital technologies, FNTIC has quickly become a hub for technological education and research in the region.

Our faculty offers cutting-edge programs that combine theoretical knowledge with practical skills in:

  • Computer Science and Software Engineering
  • Artificial Intelligence and Data Science
  • Networking and Cybersecurity
  • Telecommunications and IoT
  • Embedded Systems and Electronics
  • Cloud Computing and Distributed Systems

FNTIC boasts state-of-the-art facilities including:

  • Modern computer labs with high-performance workstations
  • Specialized laboratories for networking, electronics, and embedded systems
  • Research centers focusing on emerging technologies
  • Collaboration spaces for student projects and innovation

Through strong industry partnerships and a commitment to practical learning, FNTIC prepares students to excel in the rapidly evolving tech industry, equipping them with the skills needed to become the innovators and leaders of tomorrow.

Organizing Clubs

Elitech Club

Dedicated to fostering technological excellence through workshops, hackathons, and collaborative projects that push the boundaries of innovation. Elitech Club provides students with opportunities to develop practical skills, work on real-world challenges, and connect with industry professionals. Our members have won numerous national competitions and implemented impactful community projects.

Science Corner

Empowering students to bridge academic knowledge with practical innovations in oil & gas, IT, robotics, and emerging technologies. Science Corner organizes regular seminars, research groups, and innovation challenges to stimulate scientific thinking and technological creativity. We foster a culture of innovation through hands-on projects and industry collaboration.

Electronic Club

Focusing on electronics and hardware innovation, this club bridges the gap between software and physical computing solutions. From embedded systems to IoT devices, our members gain hands-on experience with cutting-edge hardware technologies. We organize workshops, build competitions, and collaborative projects that bring electronic innovations to life.

Featured Workshops




FIRST HOUR

SELF BRANDING

By ASALA DOB

This workshop will teach you how to build and maintain a powerful personal brand in the digital age. Learn strategies for online presence, professional networking, and personal marketing that will set you apart in the competitive tech industry.

Key topics include LinkedIn optimization, personal website creation, digital portfolio development, and effective self-presentation techniques for career advancement.

ASALA DOB

Personal Branding Expert



FIRST HOUR

From Classroom to Career

By KARIM KHELIFI

This practical workshop focuses on bridging the gap between academic learning and professional requirements. Discover how to translate your classroom knowledge into real-world skills that employers value.

Participants will learn about industry expectations, essential workplace skills, project management methodologies, and how to effectively showcase their abilities to potential employers.

KARIM KHELIFI

Tech Industry Leader




SECOND HOUR

From Home Town to the World

By FARID HADJ SAID

This inspiring session will show you how to leverage your local skills and knowledge to compete on a global scale. Learn strategies for remote work, international collaboration, and building a career without geographical limitations.

The workshop covers global tech trends, remote work platforms, cultural considerations in international teams, and how to position yourself for global opportunities while based in Algeria.

FARID HADJ SAID

International Tech Consultant




SECOND HOUR

FPGA & VHDL Programming

By ADEM MELLOUKA

This technical workshop provides hands-on experience with Field Programmable Gate Arrays (FPGAs) and VHDL programming. Perfect for students interested in hardware design, embedded systems, and digital electronics.

Participants will learn the fundamentals of FPGA architecture, VHDL syntax and structure, simulation techniques, and practical applications in modern electronics design.

ADEM MELLOUKA

Embedded Systems Engineer
