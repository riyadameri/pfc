About The Event

INNOVEX is the flagship event of the Faculty of New Technologies of Information and Communication (FNTIC) at Kasdi Merbah University, Ouargla. Now in its 8th edition, this annual exhibition and conference showcases the innovative final cycle projects of our students.

The event brings together students, professors, researchers, and industry professionals to celebrate technological innovation and excellence. With participation from Elitech Club, Electronic Club, and other student organizations, INNOVEX 2025 will feature:

  • Exhibition of cutting-edge student projects
  • Keynote speeches from industry leaders
  • Technical workshops and panel discussions
  • Networking opportunities with professionals
  • Awards ceremony for outstanding projects

This year's theme: "INNOVEX and the Student of Kasdi Merbah Who Founded It" honors the legacy of student-driven innovation at our university.

About FNTIC

The Faculty of New Technologies of Information and Communication (FNTIC) at Kasdi Merbah University, Ouargla is a leading academic institution dedicated to advancing knowledge and innovation in ICT fields.

Established to meet the growing demand for expertise in digital technologies, FNTIC offers programs that combine theoretical knowledge with practical skills in:

  • Computer Science
  • Software Engineering
  • Networking
  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Telecommunications

With modern laboratories, research centers, and industry partnerships, FNTIC prepares students to excel in the rapidly evolving tech industry.
